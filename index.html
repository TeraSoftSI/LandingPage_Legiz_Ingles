About Us

TeraSoft is a company created in order to offer digital solutions to problems and needs that arise in the contemporary world.

For this purpose, Legiz is developed in this report.

This project is a web application created with the purpose of serving as a platform that provides a communication channel between lawyers and their clients to provide solutions to legal problems, in addition to having the opportunity to provide legal advice.

In this way, Legiz aims to be a meeting point between lawyers and people who have legal problems or need legal advice of any kind.

Specializations

Human Rights

Human rights are inherent rights of all human beings, regardless of nationality, place of residence, sex, national or ethnic origin, color, religion, language, or any other condition. We all have the same human rights without discrimination. These rights are interrelated, interdependent and indivisible.

Civil Law

We offer comprehensive advice and sponsorship for the detection of possible transactional risks and in this way avoid possible contingencies that harm the interests of our clients. Likewise, our sponsorship and advice focuses on civil proceedings related to real estate properties, inheritances and wills.

Criminal Law

A criminal lawyer is a professional who has specialized in Criminal Law, a legal branch that is responsible for determining various types of crimes and establishing the penalties to which those who incur them will be subjected, in order to maintain social order.

Commercial Law

A commercial lawyer is one who is in charge of law relating to companies and businesses. On many occasions he acts as an advisor, but he is much more than that. It is the professional who will guide us in multiple processes.

International Right

An international lawyer is a professional who has specialized in International Law, either in its public aspect (which regulates relations between States and other subjects with international legal personality), or private (legal relations between individuals and that have an international character) .

Digital Law

The work of a digital lawyer or ICT specialist will be, as in other legal disciplines, to provide advice and legal representation to their private clients or companies when a conflict with their rights arises derived from legal, commercial or personal operations in which they have been used new technologies.
